(About)

I'm a Dubai based video editor exploring photography, film, and visual storytelling.

I've always been drawn to visuals, the way a single moment can feel completely different depending on how it's captured, framed, or edited. Over time, that curiosity grew into a genuine interest in photography, videography, and editing. I enjoy the entire process from capturing a frame to shaping the final story.

My work often begins in post production, where pacing, color, sound, and structure shape how a story feels. Photography and videography naturally extend that process, helping maintain a consistent visual direction from capture to the final output.

Based in Dubai, I collaborate with clients across the UAE and India on individual and commercial projects, delivering visuals that are crafted, deliberate, and built to last.
